Getting the Best Out of SearchLog

SearchLog is meant to store information and give you, and possibly your visitors, powerful access to it. You can configure it to be private, or something other people can read, or something other people can read and add material to. In other words, it can be your private tool, or a weblog, or a public collaboration tool.

Play around. See what the features are. Find out how you can use it best.
