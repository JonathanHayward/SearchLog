Thoughts About Computer Work

I need to stop measuring my work by how long I spend. There's a productive zone, and if I push and push myself outside of that productive zone, I've persevered, but I end up doing less, making more mistakes, and spending more time debugging. I need to take a break when I'm getting outside of that productive area.
