Technical: Advanced Tips

Essentially, you can add entries and search for them. You can enter in plain text (or HTML if you prefer: <a href="http://JonathansCorner.com" target="_blank">a link to Jonathan's Corner</a> is a link to Jonathan's Corner). At the bottom of each page is a 'highlight' link: type 'search' and click on 'highlight', and see the word 'search' highlighted.

There is a somewhat wiki-like way to link between pages: you can link to a title (or part of a title):

[[Getting the Best Out of SearchLog]] becomes [[Getting the Best Out of SearchLog]].

[[Getting the Best Out of SearchLog][your preferred text]] becomes a link with [[Getting the Best Out of SearchLog][your preferred text]].

Beyond that, play around and look at the options! Enjoy!
